Overview

A real-time simulation environment replicating air traffic control operations, including aircraft trajectory planning, vectoring, and conflict detection.

Key Features

  • Waypoint routing and trajectory generation for multiple aircraft.
  • Vectoring and altitude/speed control to simulate pilot-controller interaction.
  • Conflict detection and resolution enforcing separation standards.
  • Exploration of aerospace safety and human decision systems.

Technical Skills Applied

Python, Pygame for visualization, systems simulation, trajectory generation, aerospace navigation and safety-critical software development.
